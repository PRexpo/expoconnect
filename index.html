Simple Process
How ExpoConnect Works
1

Organizer Lists Expo

Create your expo page with venue, dates and stall layout. Set ticket prices or keep it free.

2

Exhibitors Book Stalls

Businesses browse slots, book online and upload products and contact details.

3

Attendees Get Tickets

Visitors discover the expo, pre-browse all exhibitors and book free or paid tickets.

4

Expo Goes Live

Everyone connects at the event. The stall directory is live for all visitors.

5

Permanent Archive

After the expo, all stall pages stay live. Customers still find exhibitors anytime.
