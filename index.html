Simple Process
How ExpoConnect Works
1

Organizer Lists Expo

Create your expo page with venue, dates, categories and stall layout. Set ticket prices or keep it free.

2

Exhibitors Book Stalls

Businesses browse slots, book online, and upload products and contact details instantly.

3

Attendees Get Tickets

Visitors discover the expo, pre-browse all exhibitors and book their tickets — free or paid.

4

Expo Goes Live

Everyone connects at the event. The stall directory is visible to all visitors in real time.

5

Permanent Archive

After the expo, all stall pages remain live. Customers can still find exhibitors and get in touch.
